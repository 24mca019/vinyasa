Meet Our Founder

Coach Deepak Tomar

Founder and Lead Instructor at Vinyasa Yoga and Pilates

With extensive training and a passion for mindful movement, Coach Deepak Tomar founded Vinyasa Yoga and Pilates to bring authentic yoga wisdom and modern fitness expertise to the heart of Ahmedabad.

Deepak's journey in yoga and fitness began with a deep commitment to personal transformation and wellness. As founder, he has developed expertise across multiple disciplines including traditional yoga, modern pilates, and fitness training, making him uniquely qualified to guide students of all levels.

As a dedicated founder and instructor with specialized training in various movement practices, Deepak creates personalized experiences that honor each student's unique journey while building strength, flexibility, and inner peace. His teaching philosophy centers on making wellness accessible and transformative for modern lifestyles.
